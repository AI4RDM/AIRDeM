About AIRDeM

In an era defined by data-driven innovation, effective Research Data Management (RDM) is vital to ensure reproducibility, integrity, and accessibility of scientific discoveries. The AIRDeM 2025 workshop focuses on leveraging Artificial Intelligence (AI) to enhance RDM practices, with particular attention to advancing FAIR (Findable, Accessible, Interoperable, Reusable) principles. By exploring AI-driven solutions such as automated metadata generation, knowledge graph integration, and FAIR compliance tools, AIRDeM aims to create practical approaches for tackling modern RDM challenges across diverse fields like life sciences, earth sciences, and digital humanities.

Call for Participation

We invite submissions for the AIRDeM workshop at the Extended Semantic Web Conference (ESWC). Participants can contribute through the following formats:

  • Research Papers: Share innovative AI-driven solutions addressing RDM challenges or enhancing FAIR practices.
  • Demos: Present real-world AI tools for improving RDM workflows.
  • Posters: Highlight novel approaches, insights, or tools through visual presentations.
  • "Bring Your Challenge": Submit real-world RDM problems to collaborate on solutions during the workshop.
